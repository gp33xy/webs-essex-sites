Donate

Please help us empower communities to take charge of their lives by donating today. With your help, OLCAP is transforming the lives of the poor in Kenya.

£30 can empower youths and women to train and begin trade with returns enough to meet basic needs for a family. £25 can pay school fees for a poor secondary school student for a term. £5 gets Malaria tested and treated in one patient at the Lukanji clinic. £30,£50 or £100 will help build a better equipped clinic to serve the poor.
