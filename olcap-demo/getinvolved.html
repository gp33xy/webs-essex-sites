Get Involved

Please help us empower communities to take charge of their lives by donating today.

WHAT CAN YOU DO?

Volunteer, fundraise, donate, help implement projects in Kenya. Get involved in a way that suits you today to reduce poverty in Kenya.

VOLUNTEER

We are always looking for volunteers with knowledge, experience and skills in project management, fundraising, proposal writing, IT (website maintenance), training etc.

What you can do as a volunteer?

1. Raise funds by taking part in events such as running or cycling on behalf of OLCAP.
2. Set up a personal JustGiving page
2. Research on funding opportunities and write proposals for funding.
3. Help implement projects in Kenya

If you wish to get involved please contact us at: info@olcap.org
Thank you for your support!
