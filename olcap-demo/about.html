About Us

Organization of Local Communities Against Poverty (OLCAP) is a national NGO legally registered by the Kenya NGOs Co-ordination board. Its main goal is to reduce the burden of poverty by securing basic rights for people living in Kenya. OLCAP supports the need for improved access to quality education and health care and promotes economic empowerment.

BACKGROUND

OLCAP was formed in 2009 by professionals who felt that there was a need for an organization owned by local communities in Kenya that would help them take charge of their lives through participation, learning, ownership and empowerment.

MISSION

Improving the quality of life of people living in Kenya.

VISION

Kenyans who are educated, healthy, self-sufficient and able to make a sustainable livelihood.

OBJECTIVES

  • To support quality primary and secondary education to children in Kenya.
  • To support Kenyans people to gain skills for income generation.
  • To reduce household poverty through the provision of credit and savings facilities to women and youths to enable the start of income generating activities.
  • To support the Primary, reproductive and sexual health needs of people, particularly women, girls and the youth by working in partnership with local clinics, and to provide health services to those affected by HIV/AIDS.
  • To secure funding for new projects and maintain existing ones
